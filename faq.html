Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does DogMeetDog do?

    Looking to find your wag or discover your bark? You’ve come to the right place: we’re crazy about companion animals. So crazy, we created DogMeetDog with several companions ready to play fetch or sunbath at you local park.

  • What is DogMeetDog mission?

    The steady success and growth of DogMeetDog has allowed us to expand our mission even further. In addition to providing dog and cat owners all the information they need to live happily and healthily with their pets, we are also giving back to the community by supporting shelter animals and promoting animal welfare. We work with several rescue organizations and have created several programs to help carry out this mission.
